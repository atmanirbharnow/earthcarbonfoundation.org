Architecting a Sustainable Legacy.

Earth Carbon Foundation is a global non-profit dedicated to restoring the delicate balance of our planet's atmosphere through science-led sequestration and equitable carbon markets.

"We don't inherit the earth from our ancestors, we borrow it from our children."

One Earth, One Family

A world where the economy operates in harmony with the biosphere. We envision a future where every community is empowered to protect their local ecosystems while benefiting from the global carbon revenue network.

Reduce and Enable

To drastically reduce global emissions and enable carbon revenue access globally, bridging the gap between high-impact climate projects and institutional investment.

  • check_circle Democratizing carbon credit accessibility
  • check_circle Developing scientific sequestration protocols

Our Journey

The Foundation

Founded by a group of environmental scientists and economists in Geneva, aiming to fix the broken voluntary carbon market.

2018

10M Tree Milestone

Successful reforestation of the Amazon Basin's degraded lands, securing our first major institutional partnership.

2021

Global Expansion

Launched the Carbon Access Portal, connecting rural smallholders in 14 countries to the international carbon market.
