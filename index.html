Frequently Asked Questions

How is the Atmanirbhar Score calculated? expand_more
The score is a weighted composite of 5 pillars: Energy Efficiency, Water Conservation, Waste Diversion, Carbon Footprint reduction, and Community Impact, validated against international ISO standards.
How do we monetize our carbon savings? expand_more
We screen your projects for eligibility in global carbon markets. Once verified via our MRV framework, your environmental assets are registered and can be sold to organizations meeting ESG requirements.
Is our data secure? expand_more
Yes, all institutional data is encrypted using AES-256 and stored on secure cloud infrastructure with strictly controlled access management.
