The software measures your annual resource consumption against your on-site sustainable generation. For example, if your campus consumes 1,00,000 kWh of grid energy but generates 65,000 kWh via rooftop solar, your Clean Energy Self-Reliance is 65%.

Your overall score is an algorithmic average across 6 core pillars:

  1. ☀️ Clean Energy (35%): Rooftop solar, LED penetration, grid independence
  2. 💧 Water Security (32%): Rainwater harvesting, wastewater recycling
  3. 🔄 Waste Circularity (25%): On-site composting, source segregation
  4. 🌳 Ecological Density (4%): Campus canopy cover, native trees
  5. 🤝 Stakeholder Activation (2%): Student-led green club engagement
  6. 💡 Climate Innovation (2%): Curricular integration, live labs
